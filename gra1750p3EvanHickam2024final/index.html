Vegan Thai Yellow Curry with Sweet Potatoes

This chickpea curry recipe with sweet potato and spinach is seasoned with fragrant spices for a yummy vegan dish. Said to be one of the favorites of yogic leader, Guru Kadi Ramaswamy, it has been served in India for nearly 400 years. Serve with basmati rice and naan bread.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas, drained
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
  • 1 sweet potato, cubed
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon red chile flakes
  • 1 cup baby spinach

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Cook onion, garlic, and ginger in hot oil until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add chickpeas, tomatoes, coconut milk, and sweet potato.
  4. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer until tender, about 15 minutes.
  5. Season with garam masala, cumin, turmeric, salt, and chile flakes. Add spinach right before serving.
